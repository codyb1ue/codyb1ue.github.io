Memozine ✍🏻

“备忘杂志” 是 Memorandum 和 Magazine 的组合词语。
记录和反思媒介，社会和文化变迁中的具体生活。还写诗和短篇小说。

"Memozine" is a combination of the words Memorandum and Magazine. It focuses on and reflects on specific lives in the media, social and cultural changes. It also publishes poetry and novel.
